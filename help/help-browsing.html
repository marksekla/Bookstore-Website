Browsing the Catalog

Our book catalog features a wide selection of titles across different genres. This guide will help you navigate and find the books you're looking for.


Accessing the Catalog

There are several ways to access our book catalog:

  • Click on the "Books" link in the main navigation menu
  • Click on "Browse Books" on the homepage
  • Click on any category card on the homepage to go directly to books in that category

Searching for Books

To search for specific books:

  1. Locate the search bar at the top of the Books page
  2. Enter a book title, author name, or keyword
  3. Press Enter or click the search button
  4. The results will display books matching your search term

Search Tips

  • You can search by partial title or author name
  • Searches are not case-sensitive
  • You can search by genre (e.g., "fantasy" or "romance")

Filtering by Category

To filter books by category:

  1. On the Books page, locate the category filter dropdown
  2. Select a category from the dropdown menu
  3. The page will update to show only books in the selected category

Viewing Book Details

To view detailed information about a book:

  1. Find a book you're interested in
  2. Click on the book title or the "Details" button
  3. This will take you to the book's detail page, which includes:
    • Cover image
    • Title, author, and category
    • Price
    • Description
    • Available options (cover type, edition)
    • Additional details and reviews

Book Options

On each book's detail page, you can customize your purchase with different options:

  • Cover Type: Choose between paperback, hardcover, leather bound, or digital
  • Edition: Select from standard, annotated, illustrated, or collector's edition
  • Quantity: Specify how many copies you want

The price will update automatically based on your selections.


Adding Books to Cart

To add a book to your shopping cart:

  1. From the catalog page, click the "Add to Cart" button on any book
  2. From a book's detail page, select your desired options and click "Add to Cart"
  3. The book will be added to your cart and the cart count will update

For more information about using the shopping cart, see Shopping Cart.
