Changing Themes

Bookworm Haven offers multiple visual themes to enhance your browsing experience and customize the look and feel of the website to your preferences.


Available Themes

We currently offer three different themes:

Default Theme

The standard light theme featuring a clean, bright design with blue accent colors. This theme is ideal for:

  • Daytime browsing
  • High readability in bright environments
  • Users who prefer traditional website designs

Dark Theme

A dark-colored theme designed to reduce eye strain in low-light environments. This theme is perfect for:

  • Nighttime browsing
  • Reducing eye fatigue
  • Conserving device battery life
  • Users who prefer darker interfaces

Seasonal Theme

A dynamic theme that automatically changes based on the current season:

  • Spring (March-May): Fresh green theme with nature-inspired elements
  • Summer (June-August): Bright orange/yellow theme with sunny visuals
  • Fall (September-November): Warm orange and brown theme with autumn colors
  • Winter (December-February): Cool blue theme with winter-inspired design

How to Change Themes

You can easily switch between themes using the theme selector in the navigation menu:

  1. Look for the dropdown menu labeled "Theme" in the top navigation bar
  2. Click on the dropdown to see the available theme options
  3. Select your preferred theme:
    • Default Theme
    • Dark Theme
    • Seasonal Theme
  4. The website will immediately update to display your selected theme

Your theme preference will be saved in your browser and will persist across different pages and future visits to the website.


Troubleshooting

If you experience issues with themes not displaying correctly:

  • Try refreshing the page after selecting a new theme
  • Make sure your browser allows cookies and local storage
  • Try using a different browser
  • If using the Seasonal theme, check that your device's date and time are set correctly

If you continue to experience issues with themes, please contact us for assistance.
